About Korea

Korea is a place where deep-rooted traditions meet fast-moving modern life. It is known for its rich history, beautiful language, meaningful customs, delicious food, advanced cities, and global cultural influence.

Korean Culture & Traditions

Hanbok

Hanbok is traditional Korean clothing known for its graceful lines, bright colors, and elegant beauty.

Respect & Etiquette

Respect for elders, politeness, and proper manners are an important part of Korean culture and daily life.

Festivals & Heritage

Korea has many traditional celebrations, palaces, temples, and customs that reflect its long and fascinating history.

Korean Food

Korean food is flavorful, comforting, and diverse. It includes spicy, savory, fermented, grilled, and street-style dishes loved by many.

Kimchi

A famous fermented side dish that is a key part of Korean meals.

Bibimbap

A mixed rice dish with vegetables, sauce, and rich flavors.

Tteokbokki

A popular spicy street food made with chewy rice cakes.

Korean BBQ

A fun and flavorful dining experience loved around the world.

Famous Places in Korea

Seoul

A vibrant capital city full of fashion, history, nightlife, and innovation.

Jeju Island

Known for its natural beauty, peaceful landscapes, and scenic views.

Gyeongbokgung Palace

A beautiful royal palace that reflects Korea’s historical grandeur.

Busan

A coastal city famous for beaches, seafood, and lively cultural spots.

Modern Korea

Modern Korea is also known for its global impact through K-pop, K-dramas, skincare, fashion, technology, and creative media. It has become a major cultural force that inspires people around the world.
